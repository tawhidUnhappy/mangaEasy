One chapter, start to finish — work through the steps top to bottom. The badge on each step shows how far along you are. Uses the manga name & chapter below; music and voice come from the Project tab.

1 Get the chapter

Got the pages from another site or scraped them yourself? Just drop the image files into the download folder — the next steps don't care where pages came from.

2 Crop into panels

Both open an editor in your browser and save the panels into the chapter's panels folder. Already have panels? Drop them in the folder and move on.

3 Write the narration

Prefer to write it yourself (or with an AI)? Create narration_01.json in the chapter folder: a JSON array of {"image": "panel file", "narration": "text"} entries.

4 Generate audio & video

Audio clones your voice reference with IndexTTS, then the video is rendered from panels + audio and your background music is mixed in. The finished MP4 lands in the chapter folder. One job runs at a time — watch the log below.
